# Ecommerce-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI
Designed a 4-page interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing e-commerce sales, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency. Built KPI-driven reports using DAX, Power Query, and data modeling techniques to generate actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.
Objectives
           Monitor sales and profit performance Analyze product category performance Understand customer behavior and demographics Track operational efficiency and return rates Enable data-driven decision makingDashboard Pages

1️⃣ Executive Summary

KPIs:Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Average Order Value,Return Rate

Visuals:
          Monthly Sales Trend
           Monthly Profit Trend
            Sales by Country
             Sales vs Profit Analysis

2️⃣ Product Category Analysis

KPIs:Total Products, Total Quantity Sold, Profit Margin %, Best Selling Category, Highest Profit Category

Visuals:
         Sales by Category
          Profit by Category
           Top Products by Sales
             Category Performance Trends

3️⃣ Customer Analytics

KPIs: Total Customers, Average Customer Spend, Average Customer Age, Top Customer Country, Highest Spending City

Visuals:
         Customers by Country
          Gender Distribution
           Revenue by Gender
             Age Group Analysis
              Monthly Customer Growth

4️⃣ Operations Analytics

KPIs:Total Orders, Return Rate %, Total Returns, Average Order Value, Best Operational Country

Visuals:
        Monthly Orders Trend
         Monthly Return Trend
          Return Rate by Category
           Return Rate by Country
            Orders vs Returns Analysis
